List of SEO Prompt 

# 1. Find Keyword Ideas for Your Primary Topic :
```text
Prepare a table of 100 keywords for the primary topic [your topic]. It should have short-tail, long-tail, LSI keywords, and transactional keywords.
```

# 2. Question Based Keyword related to Seed Keyword :
```text
Find question-based keywords related to "[your seed keyword]"
```

# 3. Content Brief for Specific Topics :
```text
Create a content brief on the topic [Topic Here]. Include “word count,” “main keyword,” “meta description,” “SEO title suggestion,” “H1 suggestions”, and “instructions for writers.”
```

# 4. Prepare Content Briefs for a List of Products :
```text
Prepare content briefs for all the [product name] in the [product category]
```

# 5. Create Conversion-Focused Content Briefs :
```text
Prepare a conversion-focused content brief containing user intent, CTAs, LSI keywords to use, persuasive words to use, and word count. The topic is [Your Topic]
```

# 6. Prepare Content Briefs for Different Buyer Journey Stages
```text
Prepare a content brief for the topic [Your Topic], including instructions on creating content according to the buyer journey stage. Similarly, create briefs for all these topics: [Include more topics here]
```

# 7. Update Your Existing Content with New Keywords
```text
Update this content to include the keywords “[add your keywords here]”: [add your content here]
```

# 8. Include Statistics in Your Content
```text
Include a list of the latest stats related to this topic: [your topic] [Mention the source of the stat in brackets. All stats should have numbers in them]
```

# 9. Generate Long-Tail Keyword-Based Questions for Your Target Query
```text
List the most popular long-tail keywords in question format for “your seed keyword.”
```

# 10. Get a List of People Also Ask (PAA) and Related Search Questions
```text
Give me a list of 10 related searches and people also ask questions for the topic [Enter your topic]
```

# 11. Create Product FAQs for Ecommerce SEO
```text
Prepare a list of 3 FAQs based on this product description [add your product description]
```

# 12. Prepare a List of FAQs Related to Misconceptions in Your Target Industry
```text
Create some FAQs that address misconceptions about [your target industry]
```

# 13. Perform Google E-E-A-T Analysis of Competitor’s Content
```text
Analyze the below content for the Google E-E-A-T ranking system for Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness. [addyour content and also include the author bio]
```

# 14. Semantic Analysis of Competitor’s Content
```text
Analyze my competitors' content for the keyword "[add your keyword]" and perform a semantic analysis. Here is the content: [share your competitor’s content]
```

# 15. Readability Analysis of Your Site Content
```text
Analyze and improve the readability of this content. [paste your content]
```
